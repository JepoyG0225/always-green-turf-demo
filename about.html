Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship. Grown on Trust.

Always Green Turf started with one belief: every homeowner deserves a beautiful yard they don't have to fight for. We built our craft in California — where water-conscious homeowners were already making the switch — and refined our process across hundreds of installations before bringing that expertise to the Phoenix Valley.

Arizona's climate demands more than most installers understand. Caliche soil, triple-digit heat, and monsoon drainage require a specific approach that goes well beyond rolling out turf and hammering nails. We built a 7-phase process around those demands.

Today we operate from Mesa, serving every corner of the Valley with a team of trained, W-2 employees — not subcontractors. 500+ installations later, our standards are the same as day one.
