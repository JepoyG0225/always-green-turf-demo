Built for Pets

Clean, Safe & Built to Handle Anything Your Pets Throw at It

Pet turf isn't just regular turf — it's engineered specifically for animals. From drainage to fiber softness, every detail is designed to keep your pets comfortable and your yard pristine.

Superior Drainage System

Our perforated backing drains up to 30 inches of liquid per hour — pet waste passes straight through so your turf stays odor-free and sanitary with a simple rinse.

Antimicrobial Infill

Zeolite and antimicrobial infill capture and neutralize ammonia odors at the molecular level — keeping your yard smelling fresh even through Arizona's hottest months.

Lead-Free & Non-Toxic

Every material we install is 100% lead-free, non-toxic, and safe for dogs, cats, and the humans who love them — certified to the highest industry standards.

Engineered for Pets

Turf Specifically Selected for Dog & Cat Owners

Not all turf is the same. We carry pet-specific product lines with shorter pile heights, higher density, and reinforced backing designed to handle the demands of active animals.

Shorter Pile Height

Easier to clean and rinse — pet waste doesn't get trapped deep in long fibers, keeping hygiene effortless.

High-Density Construction

Holds up to digging, running, and years of heavy use without matting, thinning, or losing its shape.

Cool-Touch Fibers

Paw-friendly even in Arizona summer — our materials stay significantly cooler than standard artificial grass options.

No Mud, Ever

Eliminates dirt and mud entirely — no more paw prints tracked through the house after every trip outside.

How It Works

Our Complete Pet Turf Installation Process

From first call to final walkthrough — we handle every step so you and your pets can enjoy your new yard as soon as possible.

01

Consultation

We come to you for a free on-site assessment of your space and your pets' needs — no pressure, no obligation, just honest answers.

02

Base Preparation

We remove existing material and build a durable, drainable foundation that prevents puddles and supports decades of use.

03

Turf Selection

Choose from our pet-specific product lines — each selected for softness, fiber density, and performance with active animals.

04

Professional Installation

Our certified crew handles precise seam placement, secure edging, and expert turf laying — finished clean and on schedule.

05

Odor-Control & Drainage

Antimicrobial infill is integrated into the surface, locking in odor control and maximizing drainage performance from day one.

06

Final Walkthrough

We walk you through rinsing, maintenance, and getting the most from your turf — before we leave your property.
