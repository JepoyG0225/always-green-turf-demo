Why Arizona Homeowners Are Making the Switch Fast

Natural grass in Phoenix costs $150–$300/month in water alone — before you factor in maintenance, fertilizer, pest control, and re-seeding. Artificial turf eliminates every one of those costs on day one. Here's why it's the smart choice for the Valley.

Zero Water — Stays Green Year-Round
Cut Your Water Bill by Up to 70%
Up to 70% of a typical Arizona household's water use goes outdoors. Switching to artificial turf eliminates that entirely — starting the month it's installed. Under Phoenix's Stage 1 and Stage 2 water restrictions, that's not just savings. It's compliance.
Up to 70% less water use Zero irrigation needed
Built for 110°F+ Arizona Summers
Our UV-stabilized synthetic grass fibers and heat-reduction infill systems are engineered for the Sonoran Desert. Unlike natural grass that browns and dies under Phoenix's scorching summer sun, our turf stays lush and green year-round.
UV-stabilized fibers Heat-reducing infill
Drought-Proof — and Restriction-Proof
The Colorado River is at historically low levels. Arizona's water restrictions are tightening every year. Artificial turf doesn't just save you money — it future-proofs your yard against whatever restriction level comes next. Green lawn, regardless of the drought.
No pesticides or fertilizers 100% drought-proof
Zero Maintenance — Get Your Weekends Back
No mowing. No edging. No aerating. No fertilizing. No re-seeding after monsoon season. Arizona lawn maintenance is a constant, expensive battle against your own climate. Artificial turf ends that battle permanently — an occasional rinse is all it ever needs.
No mowing No fertilizer No pest control

Our Local Installation Process

Your New Lawn — From First Call to Final Walkthrough

We've installed 500+ Arizona lawns and refined every step along the way. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you reach out to the moment you step onto your finished yard.

01

Free On-Site Consultation

A local Always Green Turf specialist visits your home anywhere in the Phoenix metro. We measure your space and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote on the spot.

02

Custom Turf Selection

We guide you through our premium turf product line — right pile height, blade style, and infill system for Arizona's climate. All products carry our 15-year UV warranty.

03

Base Preparation

Our crew removes existing vegetation, grades your Arizona caliche or sandy soil, and installs a drainage-optimized decomposed granite base — critical for desert drainage.

04

Professional Installation

Our certified crew handles precise seam placement, secure edging, and expert turf laying — finished clean and on schedule. Most Phoenix-area installs are complete in a single day.

05

Infill & Power Brush

We apply your selected infill and power-brush turf fibers to standing height, giving your new lawn that lush, natural look that stays beautiful year-round.

06

Final Walkthrough

We walk you through rinsing, maintenance, and getting the most from your turf — before we leave your property. Backed by our 15-year product warranty.
