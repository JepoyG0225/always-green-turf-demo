About Our Company

We Specialize in the Installation and
Care of Synthetic Turf

Clients use our premium-quality products in commercial areas, playgrounds, residential landscape projects, putting greens, pet areas, athletic fields, or wherever else green synthetic grass is needed. Our eco-friendly turf stays 20% cooler than other turf thanks to innovative technology and materials that keep your family, friends, and pets safe.

Get the look of freshly manicured grass without the use of gardeners or maintenance. Authentic and lush, our synthetic grass products are designed to be pet- and family-friendly, safe for the planet, and offer a realistic feel.

We offer a 10-year labor warranty and 15- to 30-year coverage on turf products. We stand behind our work and guarantee your complete satisfaction, making us the best synthetic grass and turf provider in Arizona.
