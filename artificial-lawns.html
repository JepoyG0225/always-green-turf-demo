Year-Round Green

A Lawn That Looks Perfect — Every Day of the Year

Natural grass in Arizona is a losing battle. It browns in summer, floods in monsoon season, and demands constant watering even under Stage 2 restrictions. Synthetic turf ends that cycle entirely. You get a yard that stays green and full in 115°F heat, looks stunning from the street, and never asks for a single hour of your time.

  • UV-stabilized fibers rated for sustained 110°F+ exposure — they won't fade, mat, or die
  • Eliminate irrigation entirely — save $150–$300/month on your water bill starting immediately
  • Zero maintenance required — no mowing, no fertilizing, no re-seeding, ever
