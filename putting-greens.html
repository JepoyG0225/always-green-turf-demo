How It Works

From First Sketch to First Putt — Here's Exactly What Happens

You describe your space and your game. We handle every detail — design, excavation, contouring, installation, and final inspection. Most residential greens are ready to play within 1–2 days.

01

Consultation

We visit your property for a free on-site assessment — understanding your space, your game, and your vision for the perfect backyard green.

02

Custom Design

We help you select turf speed, hole count and placement, contour style, fringe areas, and border integration — creating a personalized plan for your property.

03

Site Preparation

We excavate the area, remove existing material, and build a compacted crushed-aggregate base that forms the precise contours of your design.

04

Expert Installation

Our STC-certified crew installs the putting turf with precision seaming and cup placement — ensuring a seamless, tour-quality surface from edge to edge.

05

Finishing Touches

Paver borders, fringe turf, bunker areas, and decorative elements are added — completing the design and bringing your backyard vision to life.

06

Final Walkthrough

We inspect every inch for quality, demonstrate proper care, and hand over a putting green you'll be proud to show off and play every single day.
