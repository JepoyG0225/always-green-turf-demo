Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with your turf project.

Most residential projects are completed in 1–3 days. A standard 1,000–1,500 sq ft backyard typically takes a full day. Larger projects, putting greens, or installations with significant ground prep may take 2–3 days. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your design consultation.
We recommend being home for the start of the first day so we can confirm the layout and any details. After that, you don't need to be present. Our crew will keep the work area clean and check in with any questions. We'll do a final walkthrough with you when the job is complete.
We serve the entire Phoenix metro area, including Mesa, Scottsdale, Tempe, Chandler, Gilbert, Peoria, Glendale, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, Surprise, Fountain Hills, Cave Creek, and surrounding communities. Not sure if we cover your area? Check our full service area map or give us a call.
It can get warm in direct afternoon sun — typically 20–50°F above ambient temperature. We address this through infill selection, shade placement, and turf product choice. We also recommend a quick rinse with a hose on extremely hot days, which cools the surface quickly. During your consultation we'll walk you through the best solutions for your specific yard orientation.
Total installed cost typically ranges from $8–$20 per square foot depending on turf grade, ground prep complexity, and application type. Most Arizona homeowners spend between $10,000–$25,000 for a standard backyard. We also offer $0 down financing with 12 months no interest. Read our full pricing guide or request a site visit for an accurate quote.
We stand behind our work with a workmanship warranty on every installation, plus the manufacturer's product warranty which typically ranges from 10–15 years on premium turf. As an STC-certified installer, all of our installs meet the industry's highest installation standards, which is a requirement for many manufacturer warranties to remain valid.
