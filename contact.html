Common Questions

Questions Before You Reach Out? We've Got Answers.

Here's what most Arizona homeowners ask before scheduling their first visit. If your question isn't here, just call us — we pick up.

Most residential projects are completed in 1–3 days. A standard 1,000–1,500 sq ft backyard typically takes a full day. Larger projects, putting greens, or installations with significant ground prep may take 2–3 days. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your design consultation.
We recommend being home for the start of the first day to confirm the layout and any details. After that, you don't need to be present. Our crew will keep the area clean and check in with any questions. We'll do a final walkthrough with you when the job is complete.
We serve the entire Phoenix metro area including Mesa, Scottsdale, Tempe, Chandler, Gilbert, Peoria, Glendale, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, Surprise, Fountain Hills, and Cave Creek. Not sure? Check our full service area map or give us a call.
Total installed cost typically ranges from $8–$20 per square foot depending on turf grade, ground prep, and application type. We also offer $0 down financing with 12 months no interest. Read our full pricing guide or request a site visit for an accurate quote.
It can get warm in direct afternoon sun. We address this through infill selection, shade placement, and turf product choice. A quick rinse with a hose cools the surface rapidly. We'll walk you through the best solutions for your specific yard orientation during your consultation.
We stand behind our work with a workmanship warranty on every installation, plus the manufacturer's product warranty — typically 10–15 years on premium turf. As an STC-certified installer, all installs meet the industry's highest standards, a requirement for many manufacturer warranties to remain valid.
