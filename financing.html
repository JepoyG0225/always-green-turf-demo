Smart Financial Decision

Why Smart Arizona Homeowners Finance Their Turf

Paying cash feels responsible — but it's not always the smartest financial move. Here's why financing your turf installation makes more sense than you think.

Your Water Savings Offset the Payment Immediately
The average Phoenix homeowner saves $150–$300/month on outdoor water use after switching to artificial turf. That saving starts on day one — and for many homeowners, it covers most or all of the monthly loan payment.
Keep Your Emergency Fund Intact
A 0% interest loan costs you nothing extra. Meanwhile, your savings stay liquid for emergencies, investments, or opportunities. You're not paying a premium — you're just spreading out a payment you'd make anyway.
Add Home Value Starting Day One
Professional artificial turf adds demonstrable curb appeal and resale value. You start benefiting from the increased home value the day of installation — long before the loan is paid off.
No Waiting, No Missing Out
Saving up cash takes months or years. Meanwhile, you're spending money on water, maintenance, and living with a yard you hate. Financing lets you enjoy the upgrade today and stop wasting money immediately.
No Home Equity Risk — Ever
Unlike HELOCs or home equity loans, our financing partners offer unsecured personal loans. Your home is never used as collateral. There's no lien placed on your property.
Maintenance Savings Add Up Fast
Factor in the cost of lawn services ($100–$200/month), fertilizer, pest control, and re-seeding. Eliminating these expenses further offsets your monthly payment, accelerating your return on investment.
